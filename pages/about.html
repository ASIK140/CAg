CAg Industries

Manufacturers of Silver Impregrated Graphite Contact

CAg Industries is a partnership firm established in March 2021 for manufacturing Silver Impregnated Graphite (SIG) contacts used in 'Q' Type Relays for Railway Signalling. The product is manufactured under the specifications defined under IRS S-67-85 published by Research Design and Standard Organisation (RDSO).

The firm has its works and office located at 24 Parganas (S), Kolkata (WB) with latest and state-or-art technology. All production equipment meets RDSO standard and apt to manufacture the desired SIG contacts for Indian Railways.

Partner Profile

The firm is founded by two Indian Women Residents, Mrs. Shree Mohta & Mrs. Parineeta Binani. Mrs. Parineeta Binani, responsible for company operations having past experience in operations of manufacturing SIG contact.
