CAg Industries

Manufacturers of Silver Impregrated Graphite Contact

CAg Industries, a pioneering firm specializing in the manufacturing of Silver Impregnated Graphite Contacts, stands as a testament to years of dedication, technical expertise, and a commitment to excellence. Founded on a solid foundation of technical knowledge and experience garnered through systematic research, CAg Industries has emerged as a trusted provider of high-quality contacts for various railway signaling relay manufacturers and Indian Railways.

The journey of CAg Industries has been characterized by a relentless pursuit of technical proficiency and innovation. With a profound understanding of the intricacies of contact operation and an unwavering focus on quality, the firm has developed Silver Impregnated Graphite Contacts that are compatible with a wide range of signaling relays conforming to BRS Standards. This compatibility has been validated by a vast database of satisfied customers who rely on CAg Industries' products for critical contact applications.

At the core of CAg Industries' ethos lies a commitment to professionalism and quality of service. The driving force behind the firm's success is the proven skills and dedication of its people. With a team of technically trained and qualified professionals, CAg Industries strives to establish and maintain effective schemes for developing a foolproof quality management system. This system is implemented under the direct supervision of management, ensuring that every aspect of production meets the highest standards of quality and reliability.


CAg Industries is not content with resting on past achievements; instead, it remains a dynamic and vibrant entity focused on continuous improvement. The firm places a strong emphasis on constant upgradation of its systems, technology, and human resources to achieve and exceed the quality targets set by the organization. Through a combination of hard work and modern management techniques, CAg Industries actively promotes excellence in the quality of its products, leading to greater efficiency and profitability.
